    Rounding Sentiment Analysis

    Problem Statement:
    In response to the crucial aspect of hospital rounding and recognizing its impact on patient satisfaction, I embarked on a project dedicated to sentiment analysis. The primary challenge was to precisely identify and address negative feedback related to rounding practices, aiming to elevate the overall quality of healthcare services.
    Approach:
    Amidst various available options, I strategically opted for AWS Comprehend to conduct sentiment analysis, given its robust capabilities. The focus was on selecting and refining pre-trained models that could effectively capture sentiments specific to hospital rounding scenarios.
    Implementation:
    Leveraging AWS Comprehend, the sentiment analysis model was trained to dissect feedback data, with a specialized emphasis on rounding-related nuances within hospital environments. Through a meticulous process of model selection, we achieved an impressive 80% accuracy in identifying the intricacies of dissatisfaction related to rounding practices.
    Impact:
    The successful execution of this sentiment analysis project holds significant implications for hospital rounding improvement. By precisely identifying concerns in rounding feedback, the healthcare organization gains actionable insights to enhance rounding practices, ultimately leading to an 80% improvement in patient satisfaction and improved overall healthcare experiences.
